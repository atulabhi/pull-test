Want to learn how to deploy PostgreSQL on Kubernetes+OpenEBS?
 
Written by Murat Karslioglu, VP Solutions of OpenEBS, this blog explains how to deploy PostgreSQL on Kubernetes+OpenEBS. Very elaborately, this piece also talks about why you should deploy Postgres on Kubernetes and even lists down the prerequisites for doing the same.
The blog acts as a step by step guide for the deployment of PostgreSQL. So if you have ever asked yourself this very question, please read the blog and don't forget to give us your feedback!
